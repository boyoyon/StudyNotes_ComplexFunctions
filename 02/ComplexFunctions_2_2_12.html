2.2 Cauchy-Riemann の方程式

問3

\(z=x+iy\) を \((r,\theta)\) により極表示するとき, Cauchy-Riemann の微分方程式は \[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial r}=\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial v}{\partial \theta},　\frac{\partial v}{\partial r}=-\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \] となることを示せ。

(解答)

・\(f(z)\) を極形式で表現すると \(f(z)=u\left(x(r,θ),y(r,θ)\right)+iv\left(x(r,θ),y(r,θ)\right)\)

・\(u,v\) と \(r,θ\) の関係

\[ \begin{array}{c|c|c} & u & v \\ \hline r & \displaystyle \frac{\partial u}{\partial r}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\frac{\partial x}{\partial r}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\frac{\partial y}{\partial r}\cdots ① & \displaystyle \frac{\partial v}{\partial r}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\frac{\partial x}{\partial r}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\frac{\partial y}{\partial r}\cdots ②\\ \hline θ & \displaystyle \frac{\partial u}{\partial θ}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\frac{\partial x}{\partial θ}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\frac{\partial y}{\partial θ}\cdots ③ & \displaystyle \frac{\partial v}{\partial θ}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\frac{\partial x}{\partial θ}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\frac{\partial y}{\partial θ} \cdots ④ \end{array} \]
・\(x,y\) と \(r,θ\) の関係 \[ \begin{array}{c|c|c} & r & θ \\ \hline x=r\cos θ & \displaystyle \frac{\partial x}{\partial r}=\cos θ\cdots ⑤ & \displaystyle \frac{\partial x}{\partial θ}=-r\sin θ\cdots ⑥ \\ \hline y=r\sin θ & \displaystyle \frac{\partial y}{\partial r}=\sin θ\cdots ⑦ & \displaystyle \frac{\partial y}{\partial θ}=r\cos θ\cdots ⑧ \end{array} \]
・①②③④ に ⑤⑥⑦⑧ を代入 \[ \begin{array}{c|c|c} & u & v \\ \hline r & \displaystyle \frac{\partial u}{\partial r}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos θ+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\sin θ\cdots ⑨ & \displaystyle \frac{\partial v}{\partial r}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\cos θ+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\sin θ\cdots ⑩\\ \hline & \displaystyle \frac{\partial u}{\partial θ}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cdot(-r\sin θ)+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\cdot r\cos θ & \displaystyle \frac{\partial v}{\partial θ}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\cdot(-r\sin θ)+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\cdot r\cos θ \\ & ↓両辺をrで割る & ↓両辺をrで割る \\ θ& \displaystyle \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial u}{\partial θ}=-\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\sin θ+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\cos θ & \displaystyle \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial v}{\partial θ}=-\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\sin θ+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\cos θ \\ & ↓ｺｰｼｰ･ﾘｰﾏﾝの関係式 & ↓ｺｰｼｰ･ﾘｰﾏﾝの関係式 \\ & \displaystyle \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial u}{\partial θ}=-\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\sin θ-\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\cos θ \cdots ⑪ & \displaystyle \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial v}{\partial θ}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\sin θ+\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos θ\cdots ⑫ \\ \end{array} \] ・⑨と⑫, ⑩と⑪を比較すると \[ \frac{\partial u}{\partial r}=\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial v}{\partial \theta},　\frac{\partial v}{\partial r}=-\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \] となっている。
